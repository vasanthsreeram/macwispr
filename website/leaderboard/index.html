Separate opt-in — more data than reliability telemetry. Anonymous product telemetry (off by default) only sends content-free reliability events (hotkey health, dictation completed/failed with latency/duration buckets). It does not send your name, total words dictated, how often you use the app, or WPM-style ranks. Those live on-device in the Weekly Time Saved dashboard today.

A public board needs extra fields on purpose: a display name/handle, word-count aggregates, usage frequency (e.g. dictations, streak), and derived time-saved / speed-style stats for ranking. That requires its own explicit opt-in in Settings (off by default) — turning on reliability telemetry alone will never put you here. Still never: transcript text or audio. See Privacy · tracking #11.
